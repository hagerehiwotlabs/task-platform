### 1. Radical Transparency
- **No hidden work**: All tasks tracked in GitHub Issues

- **Continuous improvement**: Always looking to improve

- **Critique code, never the coder**: Focus on solutions
- **Assume positive intent**: Everyone wants to do good work
- **Inclusive communication**: No jargon, be clear and kind
- **Celebrate wins**: Small and large victories matter
- **Listen actively**: Understand before being understood

## ‚öñÔ∏è Conflict Resolution

### Step 1: Direct Conversation (24 hours)
- Private discussion between parties
- Focus on facts and feelings using "I" statements
- Seek mutual understanding, not winning
- Document agreed actions

### Step 2: Mediation (48 hours if unresolved)
- Involve team lead as neutral mediator
- Structured conversation with agenda
- Each party gets uninterrupted time
- Document agreed actions and follow-up

### Step 3: Team Input (72 hours if unresolved)
- Present to team (anonymous details)
- Anonymous feedback collected
- Team decision on path forward
- Document decision and reasoning

### Step 4: Escalation (1 week if unresolved)
- Formal review by organization
- External mediation if needed
- Final decision documented
- Support for all parties

## üìú Code of Conduct

### Expected Behavior
- Use respectful, inclusive language
- Provide constructive, specific feedback
- Maintain professional, reliable communication
- Foster collaborative, team-first mindset
- Respect boundaries and personal time

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Public shaming or blame games
- Deliberate obstruction or sabotage
- Dishonesty or misrepresentation
- Violation of privacy or confidentiality

### Reporting Process
1. Report to team lead (or any lead if involving team lead)
2. Confidential investigation within 24 hours
3. Appropriate action taken based on evidence
4. Protection for reporter from retaliation
5. Follow-up with all parties involved
